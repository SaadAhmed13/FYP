Żabin Rybacki [ˈʐabin rɨˈbat͡ski] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Banie Mazurskie, within Gołdap County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland, 500 metres (547 yd) from the border with the Kaliningrad Oblast of Russia.
Before 1945, the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
