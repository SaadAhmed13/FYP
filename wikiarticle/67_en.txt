Jarczewo [jarˈt͡ʂɛvɔ] (German: Jarsen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Skępe, within Lipno County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Skępe, 10 km (6 mi) east of Lipno, and 49 km (30 mi) east of Toruń.
