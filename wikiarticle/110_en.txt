Janówek [jaˈnuvɛk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wierzbno, within Węgrów County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Wierzbno, 17 km (11 mi) south-west of Węgrów, and 58 km (36 mi) east of Warsaw.
